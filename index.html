InvestMenter Tracking & Referral Program

Detailed breakdown of our advanced UTM tracking system for new and existing leads - ensuring fair compensation and motivation for all partners.

System Overview: New vs Existing Leads

1

Fully New Leads

A lead who has never engaged with any of our systems — completely new to InvestMenter. The First Touch Point can only be secured if you are the very first to introduce them to us.

Method A: Active Referral Opt-in

Submit leads through: https://www.investment-kongress.com

• Automatic UTM tracking is applied upon submission

• You actively opt somebody in

• You secure the First Touch Point (FTP) forever

Method B: UTM Tracked Links

Share tracked links to: Webinars, Investment Congress, free online reports, PDFs, etc.

• Lead clicks on your tracked link and opts in

• You are forever the First Touch Point (FTP)

Example:
Dr. Max Müller brings new lead → UTM Source is forever "Dr. Max Müller" - whenever this lead appears again, we'll see forever that the FTP is Dr. Max Müller
2

Existing Leads

If a lead already exists in CRM, the First Touch Point remains unchanged — it cannot be claimed again from another source since we already have that lead. This ensures fairness for everyone and motivates each partner to bring in fresh new leads. What does get updated, however, is the Last Touch Point, which reflects the most recent interaction with the lead..

Active Referral Attempt

• System blocks submission: "This contact is already in the system"

• You receive instant email notification of the attempt

• First Touch Point (FTP) remains protected and unchanged

UTM Tracked Link Clicked

• Works exactly like the referral program

• First Touch Point (FTP) stays protected (can never change)

• You claim the Last Touch Point (LTP) position

• No email notification (for high-volume partners sending thousands of leads)

Example:
Ute Schmidt engages existing lead → LTP switches to "Ute Schmidt" in our system, but FTP stays "Dr. Max Müller"

Core System Principles

🔒 Ultimate Fairness

One lead can never be claimed from another source again - protecting original referrers and staying fair to everybody. The First Touch Point is permanently protected once secured.

🚀 Maximum Motivation

System ensures everybody is as motivated as they can get to bring in fresh new leads while rewarding re-engagement through the Last Touch Point mechanism.

⚡ Advanced UTM Tracking

Not special but advanced online marketing UTM tracking ensures accurate attribution and commission distribution with automatic source tracking.

🎯 System Summary

For Fully New Leads: You secure the First Touch Point forever by being the first to bring them to InvestMenter through either direct referral or UTM tracked links.

For Existing Leads: The First Touch Point remains protected, but you can claim the Last Touch Point through UTM tracked engagement.

Commission Distribution: When a Last Touch Point buys a property, commission splits 50/50 between First and Last Touch Points, unless the contact already took action before the Last Touch Point was claimed.

Protection Guarantee: One lead can never be claimed from another source again, ensuring ultimate fairness while maximizing motivation for bringing fresh leads.
